ROADMAP

The Timeline

Here are all past, ongoing and upcoming events organized by our club in offline and online modes

GameFlix 2.0

22 Feb, 2024

An epic six-hour adventure that seamlessly blends the thrill of Netflix series with exciting gaming challenges. Our event, inspired by the gripping narratives of "13 Reasons Why," "Peaky Blinders," and "One Piece," promises a unique and immersive experience divided into three captivating rounds.

TechAstra’23

14 Dec, 2023

TechAstra’23 -a thrilling event with lined up competitions in which the participant teams competed with each other while improving their overall knowledge. The participants faced each other in different clashes like buzzer round, JAM session, debate and picture story narration with judgment by the audience through live voting

ऐlaan

3rd and 4th July, 2023

“ऐlaan” - a two day long mega event with leading and inspiring personalities with their expertise in the field of technology and business.
The event started with insightful talks from reverd guests like Arsh Goyal, Naman Dekhmukh, Rohit Negi, Aaryan Tripathi and Ashutosh Pratihast. It was then followed by ‘SpaceBound’, a space adventure, a thrilling puzzle game involving fun tasks and riddles.

TechSaga

April 14 - April 16, 2023

TechSaga- a saga of tech and career building. Hosted eminent speakers from around the country to guide students regarding the domain of iOS development, how to rock on LinkedIn, and how to get ahead in your placement journey. It was a three-day event filled with exclusive guidance, insights and doubt-clearing sessions.

GameFlix

9 Feb, 2023

GameFlix is iOS Club’s flagship event at AdVITya. A 6-hour long fun and challenging series of games that consists of the three rounds:
Round 1: Money Heist
Round 2: Squid Game
Round 3: Wednesday

Sampurna

Jan 31 - Feb 1, 2023

A 3-day Offline Business Hackathon Event - ‘Sampurna’. Sampurna brings together more than 500 of the brightest minds from various domains to solve real-world business problems and foster a collaborative and innovative environment.

Inaugural Ceremony

Nov 11, 2022

It was the first event organized by iOS Club at VIT Bhopal. The event was graced by the honorary Chief Guest, Kashish Sharma, who is currently working as an iOS intern at automobile giant - Mercedes Benz. The ceremony was followed by HiJack-iT, a fun code-breaker competition.
